About Me

Hello! My name is Aaron Piando. I am a proud second-generation Filipino American, and I was born and raised in McAllen, Texas.
I am especially passionate about engineering data-driven software solutions to real-world business problems and social issues.
Currently, I am senior at Boston University, where I am pursuing my B.A. in Computer Science and Economics with a minor in Mathematics.
I am excited to bring my experience in software development, machine learning, project management, and academic research to the workforce.
In my free time, I enjoy traveling to new places, running around Boston, and cheering on my favorite softball and volleyball teams!
